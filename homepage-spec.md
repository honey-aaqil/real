# DR Horton NC Homes — Homepage Redesign Specification

This document outlines the required structure, sections, trust signals, and SEO guidelines for the flagship homepage redesign of the **D.R. Horton North Carolina New Construction Buyer Portal**.

---

## 1. Header / Navigation
Unlike the PPC landing pages (which drop navigation for focus), the Homepage serves as the central hub and requires a full, intuitive navigation system.
- **Logo**: Sleek luxury styling ("Spatial Portal" or "DR Horton NC Buyer Specialist").
- **"Towns" Dropdown**: Multi-column or elegant dropdown listing all 11 markets across North Carolina (grouped by tier).
- **Communities Link**: Direct navigation to general communities search/index.
- **Buyer's Guide**: Link to the downloadable PDF/Lead-magnet.
- **Incentives**: Live monthly deals and builder promos.
- **Reviews**: Social proof and buyer testimonials.
- **Contact**: Fast contact/schedule form link.
- **Direct Phone**: Clear, readable click-to-call phone number in the header.
- **CTA Button**: Sleek, high-contrast action button in the top right (e.g., "Schedule Consultation").

---

## 2. Hero (Above the Fold)
The hero must establish instant, high-perceived authority and outline the core value proposition.
- **Headline**: Naming the builder, region, and primary benefit.
  * *Example*: `"D.R. Horton New Construction Buyer Specialist — Greater Charlotte & Lake Norman"`
- **Subheadline**: The core one-sentence value pitch:
  * *"I represent buyers at D.R. Horton communities across Charlotte and Lake Norman at no cost to you — the builder pays my fee — and I protect your interests the builder’s on-site agent never will."*
- **Primary CTA Button**: Large, thumb-reach optimized button.
  * *Text*: `"Get My Free Home List"`
- **Visuals**: A high-contrast, cinematic blend of Lake Norman's tranquil landscapes and Charlotte's modern urban skylines (integrated with GSAP motion or parallax transitions).

---

## 3. Trust Bar
A high-trust strip directly below the hero section to immediately overcome buyer resistance.
- **Rating**: Stars and verified review count (e.g., "4.9/5 stars across 120+ client reviews").
- **Credentials**: "Licensed NC Realtor®".
- **Local Connection**: "Local since [Year]" or "Expert NC Construction Advisors".

---

## 4. Town Selector Grid
The homepage's primary routing job is letting visitors self-select their market. This section implements the internal-linking structure where the homepage routes traffic down to the 11 town hubs, which in turn link back to home.
- **Organization**: Grouped into three distinct regional/pricing tiers:
  1. **Tier 1 — Metro Core**: Charlotte, Ballantyne, Concord, Indian Trail
  2. **Tier 2 — Lake Norman**: Huntersville, Mooresville, Denver, Cornelius Corridor
  3. **Tier 3 — Emerging / Exurban**: Statesville, Troutman, Sherrills Ford, Lincolnton
- **Cards**: Rich cards for each town, displaying a scenic background image (city or lake), starting price range, available community counts, and a direct link to that town's hub page.

---

## 5. "Why Buy with a Free Buyer's Agent" Section
A regional, high-conversion section that clarifies agency representation in new construction.
- **The Core Concept**: The builder's on-site sales agent represents the builder's interests (maximizing price and minimizing builder liability). A buyer's agent represents the *buyer's* interests (negotiating upgrades, monitoring construction, protecting deposit), and their service is 100% free because the builder pays their commission.
- **Layout**: Clean two-column split: builder-on-site agent vs. independent buyer specialist.

---

## 6. Featured Communities & Incentives Strip
Adds dynamic freshness and targets deal-hunting buyers.
- **Highlights**: Carousel or grid of 3–4 featured D.R. Horton subdivisions (e.g., *Reedy Creek Preserve*, *Shepherds Farm*).
- **Incentives Node**: A callout pointing to the current month's finance promotions, rate locks, and closing cost assistance (linking directly to `/dr-horton-incentives/` pages).

---

## 7. Buyer's Guide Lead Magnet
Low-friction email-only capture for top-of-funnel visitors who aren't ready to speak to an agent yet.
- **Asset**: *"7 Things the Builder's On-Site Agent Won't Tell You"* PDF guide.
- **Form**: Email-only input field, clean layout, bulleted list of high-value takeaways from the guide.

---

## 8. Social Proof & Reviews
- **Content**: 2–3 detailed reviews from buyers who successfully purchased new-construction homes in the Charlotte/Lake Norman area through our specialists.
- **Trust Badges**: Google reviews badge, local Realtor badge.

---

## 9. Soft-Offer CTA
- **Concept**: A low-pressure alternative to direct forms.
- **Offer**: `"Book a free 15-minute strategy call"` via Calendly, perfect for relocating buyers wanting a quick verbal briefing on schools, commute times, or financing.

---

## 10. Footer
The master local-SEO footprint.
- **Town Index**: Direct, keyword-friendly list of all 11 town hub pages.
- **NAP Info**: Name, Address, Phone, Brokerage compliance data.
- **Utility Links**: About, Reviews, Blog, Contact, Privacy Policy, Fair Housing compliance.

---

## Core Operational Rules
1. **Mobile-First Single Column**: Designed and optimized for small viewports, ensuring all CTAs and forms are comfortably reachable with a user's thumb.
2. **Organic/Brand-Only Traffic**: This homepage is for brand queries and SEO crawling. **Never point PPC (Google Ads) traffic here**—paid campaigns must always land on their highly specific, intent-matched town hub page (e.g., `/dr-horton-mooresville-nc/`).
